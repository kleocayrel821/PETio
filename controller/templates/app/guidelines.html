Controller & Device Guidelines

Quick steps for pairing, schedules, feeding, and troubleshooting.

Pair Your Device

  1. Go to My Devices.
  2. Tap Claim a New Device.
  3. Enter your Hardware Key (UUID) or your Device ID + 6-digit PIN.
  4. After claiming, refresh My Devices and confirm the device shows Online.
Open My Devices Claim Device

Use Feed Now

  • Use Feed Now for manual feeding outside the schedule.
  • Wait for the command to finish before sending another.
  • Check Pending Commands if a command looks stuck.
Open Dashboard Pending Commands

Schedules

  • Create schedules for consistent feeding times.
  • Use realistic portion sizes and adjust slowly.
  • After editing schedules, confirm your device is online so it can sync changes.
Open Schedules

Logs & History

  • Use History to review feeding activity and timestamps.
  • If the device feed happens but no log appears, check connectivity and try again.
Open History

Troubleshooting

Device shows Offline
  • Check power and Wi-Fi signal near the feeder.
  • Restart the device and wait 30–60 seconds.
  • Open My Devices and refresh status.
Commands stuck in Pending
  • Confirm device is online.
  • Wait for one command to complete before sending another.
  • Use Pending Commands to review what is queued.
Portion looks wrong
  • Calibrate your device if your model supports it.
  • Adjust portions gradually and verify with History.
Safety tips
  • Keep the food container dry and clean.
  • Do not overfeed; follow your veterinarian’s guidance.
