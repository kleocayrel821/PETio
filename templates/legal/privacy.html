Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

PETio values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use PETio's Controller, Social, and Marketplace features.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as username, email address, and profile information.
  • Pet-related information you provide, including pet profile and feeding settings.
  • App activity such as posts, messages, listings, and device interaction logs.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve PETio features and services.
  • Enable account security, moderation, and abuse prevention.
  • Support communication, notifications, and troubleshooting.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information when required by law, to protect users, or to operate core PETio services.

4. Data Retention and Security

We keep information only as long as needed for service operation, legal compliance, and account management. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect your data.

5. Your Choices

  • You may update profile information through your account settings.
  • You may request account-related assistance via petio.ph@gmail.com.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at petio.ph@gmail.com.
